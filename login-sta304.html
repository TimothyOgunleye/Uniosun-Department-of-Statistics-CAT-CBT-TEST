Statistical Computing III

STA 304 (2 Units)  ·  Statistics, Mathematics and Geology

Important instructions to all students

1

Eligibility

  • This Mid-Semester Examination is strictly for duly registered 300-Level students offering STA 304 in the Department(s) of Statistics.
  • Only students whose details have been registered on the University's Mid-Semester Examination portal are eligible to participate.
  • Unauthorized persons will not be granted access to the test under any circumstance.
2

Access to the virtual examination hall

  • Your Matriculation Number is your unique entrance credential into the virtual hall.
  • Enter your matriculation number exactly as officially registered with the University.
  • Incorrect entry may prevent access to the Mid-Semester Examination.
  • Each matriculation number is uniquely linked to one candidate and can only be used by the registered student.
  • This CAT system is programmed to prevent unauthorized access; no person outside the approved class list can gain entry.
3

Structure of the examination

  • Total number of questions: 60 - Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs).
  • Marks per question: 0.5 Mark  ·  Total obtainable marks: 30 Marks.
  • Every question carries equal weight. Candidates are advised to attempt all questions.
4

Examination duration

  • The examination duration is 30 minutes only.
  • The timer begins immediately you click the "Start Test" button and cannot be paused, stopped, or restarted.
  • When time expires, the system automatically submits your responses. No additional time will be granted.
5

One attempt only

  • Every student is entitled to only one attempt. Once commenced, the attempt is considered active.
  • After submission - manual or automatic - you will no longer have access to the test.
  • Refreshing the browser, logging out, closing the page, or switching devices after the test has begun may terminate your session.
  • Under no circumstance shall a second attempt be granted.
6

Submission of responses

  • Review your answers carefully before clicking Submit - once submitted, responses become final and cannot be modified or recalled.
  • If time expires before submission, the system will automatically submit your chosen answers.
7

Randomization of questions

  • An automated random question selection system is used to ensure fairness and integrity.
  • Questions and the arrangement of answer options presented to one student will differ from another.
  • Every candidate receives an equivalent assessment drawn randomly from the approved question bank - students are discouraged from comparing answers during the test.
8

Internet connectivity

  • Candidates are solely responsible for reliable internet connectivity before beginning the test.
  • Before clicking "Start Test", ensure a stable connection, sufficient battery or power source, and strong network coverage.
  • Do not begin the test unless fully satisfied with your connection quality.
9

Recommended devices

  • Desktop computer, Laptop computer (highly recommended), Tablet, or Smartphone with an updated web browser.
  • Before starting: close unnecessary applications, disable automatic updates, update your browser, and keep your device connected to power where possible.
By entering your matriculation number and clicking "Start Test", you confirm that you have read, understood, and agreed to comply with all the Mid-Semester Examination rules and regulations stated above. We wish you an excellent performance.
